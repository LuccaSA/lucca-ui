Entering animations

Base syntax

To animate an element, add the .lui.animated classes.

Animation type

There are 3 kinds of entering animations:

  • .fade.in
  • .slide.in
  • .scale.in
You can also specify the direction of the animation:
  • .up
  • .down
  • .left
  • .right
